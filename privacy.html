Privacy Policy

Percolate Studio — Last updated: May 25, 2026

1. Overview

Percolate Studio is an automated video content publishing pipeline. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our website at mysteryme8.github.io and our automated publishing activities on platforms including TikTok, YouTube, and X.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

This website does not collect personal information from visitors. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics scripts, or any other mechanism to identify or profile individual users. We do not operate user accounts, mailing lists, or contact forms on this site.

3. Platform API Access

To publish content, Percolate Studio connects to social media platforms via their official APIs using developer credentials we own. During this process:

  • We authenticate using OAuth tokens scoped to content publishing only.
  • We do not access, store, or process any data belonging to platform users other than ourselves.
  • We do not read followers' profiles, private messages, or any personal data accessible through platform APIs.
  • API tokens are stored locally and are never shared with third parties.

4. Content We Publish

Published videos are generated automatically from publicly available news sources. No personal data about private individuals is intentionally included in published content. If you believe published content includes your personal information in error, please contact us at the address below.

5. Third-Party Platforms

Content published through Percolate Studio appears on third-party platforms (TikTok, YouTube, X). Each platform has its own privacy policy governing how they handle viewer data. We encourage you to review those policies directly:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Google / YouTube Privacy Policy
  • X Privacy Policy

6. Data Retention

Because we do not collect personal data from website visitors or platform users, there is no personal data for us to retain or delete. Operational logs (e.g., publish timestamps, API responses) are stored locally on our systems and do not contain personal information about third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

This service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above will reflect any changes. We encourage periodic review of this page.

9. Contact

Questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy may be directed to contact@percolatestudio.app.
